# WebNova – Creative Agency Website

## Project Overview

**Project Name:** WebNova  
**Type:** Static Website  
**Deployment Target:** GitHub Pages (direct push)  
**Location:** Germany (current) → Expanding across the European Union soon  
**Primary Languages:** English + German (bilingual)  
**Industry:** Creative Agency (Branding, Digital Design, Campaigns, Storytelling)

---

## Vision & Positioning

WebNova is a modern creative agency based in Germany with a bold, precise, and innovative approach. The website should reflect **German precision meets fearless creativity**. 

Design direction: Minimal yet visually powerful, elegant typography, smooth animations, and a premium feel that builds trust with international clients.

---

## Site Structure

### Main Pages

1. **Home** (`/`)
   - Hero Section with strong headline and CTA
   - What We Do (Services overview)
   - Selected Works (Featured projects)
   - Why WebNova (Values & Differentiation)
   - Clients / Testimonials
   - Final CTA + Footer

2. **About** (`/about`)
   - Our Story
   - Vision & Mission
   - Team profiles
   - Locations (Germany + EU expansion)

3. **Services** (`/services`)
   - Brand Strategy & Identity
   - Digital Design & UI/UX
   - Website & Digital Product Development
   - Creative Campaigns
   - Motion Design & 3D
   - Content Creation & Storytelling
   - Creative Consulting

4. **Work / Portfolio** (`/work`)
   - Filterable grid (All, Branding, Digital, Campaign, Motion, etc.)
   - Project cards with hover effects
   - Link to individual case studies

5. **Journal / Insights** (`/journal`) – Optional but recommended

6. **Contact** (`/contact`)
   - Contact Form
   - Office information
   - Get in touch section

---

## Technology Stack (Static-First)

- **Static Site Generator:** Astro (strongly recommended) or Hugo
- **Styling:** Tailwind CSS
- **Interactivity:** Alpine.js + GSAP for smooth animations
- **i18n:** Built-in internationalization (English + German)
- **Performance:** Optimized images (WebP), lazy loading, minimal JavaScript
- **Forms:** Formspree, Netlify Forms, or similar
- **SEO:** Meta tags, Open Graph, sitemap, structured data
- **Analytics:** Privacy-friendly (Umami or Plausible)

---

## Design & Visual Identity

- **Color Palette:** Deep black, clean white, with one strong accent color (e.g., electric blue or vibrant teal)
- **Typography:** Modern sans-serif (Inter + secondary premium font)
- **Style:** Modern minimalism with bold accents, subtle scroll animations, and high-quality visuals
- **Mobile:** Fully responsive, touch-friendly
- **Dark Mode:** Default (with optional light mode)

---

## Key Features

- Fully static (no backend required)
- Blazing fast performance
- Excellent SEO
- Bilingual support
- Smooth scroll animations
- Filterable portfolio
- Accessible (WCAG compliant)
- Easy to maintain and update
- Ready for custom domain
